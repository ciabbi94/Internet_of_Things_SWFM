Smart water flows monitoring.

The new way to monitor water flows and live quite!

SWFM ranks as a diruptive innovation in water flows monitoring marketplace.

The project presented hereafter aims to offer a complete solution to monitor and control water flows. The proposed solution is SWFM, a new smart way to monitor waters thanks to modern sensors placed in strategic points of rivers and ditches. Moreover, we offer the possibility to act directly on the rivers giving the possibility to monitor the state of the already present dams.
